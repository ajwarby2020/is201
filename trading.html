There are a few things that I would say are very crucial when trading.

  1. Timing

    2. One of the most important things while trading is timing. You need to time the market and the entry points. In the picture below, you can see that if you invested everything into the S&P 500 in 2000, then you would only have gained 120% on your money through 2019. If you timed your entry points, such as in 2003 and in 2008, you could ove had a 465% of return on your money. You could have avoided all the down turns and only benefited from the majr upswings.

  2. Margin
    • Stocks (2x Margin)
    • Options (10x Margin)
    • Futures (20x Margin)
    • Forex (50x Margin)

    3. Their are a few different trading assets with different available margin rates. The more margin you have available to you, the larger the buying power. This allows you to increase you profitability. But be careful, this can also increase your losses

  3. Risk Management

    4. Understanding the you will lose is part of trading. But know that you can control how much you loss. By using a tool called a stop loss, you protect yourself from losing more than you have predetermined you tolerance to be. One of my favorite quotes is "Live to Trade another day".
